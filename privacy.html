Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Qart ("we," "us," or "our") is a mobile app that helps you make more intentional purchasing decisions. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

By using Qart, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you have questions, contact us at feedback@myqart.ai.

1. Information We Collect

Account information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and a hashed password. If you sign in with Apple, we receive an anonymized Apple user identifier instead of your email.

Screenshots and images: When you add an item to Qart, you share a screenshot with us. We store that image and use it to extract product details (item name, price, retailer) via optical character recognition (OCR).

Purchase and item data: We store the items you add to Qart, including item name, price, retailer, product URL, your cooling-off period setting, your decisions (purchase, pass, or delay), and any optional notes or reflections you write.

Device information: We collect your device's push notification token (via Firebase Cloud Messaging) to send you reminders when your cooling-off periods expire.

Usage data: We collect basic logs for error tracking and performance monitoring, including API request times and error rates.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate the app and provide its core features
  • To parse screenshots and extract product details via OCR
  • To send push notifications when your cooling-off periods expire or quarterly reviews are due
  • To generate your personal spending patterns and insights
  • To generate affiliate links when you decide to purchase an item, which may earn us a commission at no cost to you
  • To improve the app and fix bugs

3. Affiliate Links

When you decide to purchase an item, Qart may redirect you through an affiliate link. This means we may earn a small commission from the retailer if you complete a purchase. The price you pay is never affected. We log affiliate link clicks for analytics purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate Qart:

  • Railway — cloud hosting for our backend and database
  • Cloudinary — image storage for your screenshots and product images
  • Firebase (Google) — push notification delivery
  • Google Cloud Vision API — OCR to extract product details from screenshots
  • Anthropic (Claude API) — fallback OCR when Google Cloud Vision is unable to parse an image

Each of these services has its own privacy policy and data practices. We only share the minimum data necessary for each service to function.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data and Qarted items within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal or business purposes (such as affiliate commission records).

6. Data Security

We use industry-standard security practices including HTTPS encryption for all data in transit, bcrypt password hashing, and JWT-based authentication. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt out of push notifications at any time via your device settings

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at feedback@myqart.ai.

8. Children's Privacy

Qart is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of Qart after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at feedback@myqart.ai.
